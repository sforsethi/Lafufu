About Lafufu

Lafufu is the ultimate mobile companion for collectible toy enthusiasts. Our app helps you discover, track, and showcase your adorable Labubu toy collection across multiple themed series.

Whether you're collecting fruit-themed figures, anime collaborations, or limited edition releases, Lafufu provides an intuitive and beautiful way to manage your collection and connect with fellow collectors.

With features like collection tracking, series exploration, and social sharing, Lafufu makes collecting more enjoyable and organized than ever before.
